How to Start Studying When Overwhelmed

Feeling overwhelmed before studying is normal. The problem is not the work itself — it’s the mental weight of thinking about everything at once.

Stop Thinking About Everything

When you look at the entire syllabus, your brain freezes. Instead, zoom in on just ONE small topic.

Start Ridiculously Small

Don’t try to “study”. Just open your notebook. Then read only one page.

Starting is the real challenge — not studying itself.

The 10-Minute Entry Rule

Promise yourself you will study for only 10 minutes. After that, you can stop. Most of the time, you won’t stop.

Remove Pressure

You don’t need to finish everything. You only need to begin.

Use Instant Action

If you feel stuck and don’t know what to do first, Instant Action gives you a simple next step based on your situation.
