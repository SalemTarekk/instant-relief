How to Stop Overthinking

Overthinking happens when your mind keeps analyzing a situation without moving toward action. It creates stress, confusion, and delays decisions.

Why You Overthink

Most overthinking comes from wanting the “perfect” decision. But in real life, perfect clarity rarely exists before action.

Shift From Thinking to Doing

Instead of trying to solve everything in your mind, focus on one small action you can take right now.

Use the 1-Step Rule

Ask yourself: “What is the next tiny action I can do in under 2 minutes?” Then do only that.

Accept Imperfect Decisions

You don’t need perfect answers. You only need enough clarity to take the next step.

Use Instant Action

If you feel stuck in your thoughts, Instant Action turns your situation into one clear action step.
